# Content Pillars — All 6 Thematic Areas

## Pillar Structure
Each pillar follows this architecture:
- 3-4 Core Tensions (the fundamental debates)
- FOR arguments (2-3 per tension) with evidence at Tier 1 (SG) and Tier 2 (International)
- AGAINST arguments (2-3 per tension) with same evidence structure
- 10 Singapore examples
- 10 International examples
- Cross-pillar connections
- 6 practice questions
